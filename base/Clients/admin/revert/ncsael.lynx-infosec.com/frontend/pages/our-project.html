Welcome to our project "ReCyP:HER"

Rethinking Cybersecurity in Pakistan – Human factors‘ Essential Role (RECYPHER) is a project which is being funded by the European Union (EU) within the Erasmus+ programme.

The goal is to create a comprehensive capacity building in Pakistan for the area of cybersecurity and the connection with psychological know-how.
