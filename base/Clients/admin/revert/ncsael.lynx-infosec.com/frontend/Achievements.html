NCSAEL PUBLISHES RESEARCH WORK IN World's most prestigeous Journal of Network and Computer Applications (IF 5.273)

NCSAEL has published its research work "Trust model of internet of smart things: A Survey, open issues and future directions" authored by Ayesha Altaf, Director NCSAEL, Dr. Haider Abbas and Faiza Iqbal in World's most prestigious Computer Journal of Network and Computer Applications (IF 5.273). This paper presents a comprehensive overview of existing surveys on trust models of IoT. It provides classification of Trust Related Attacks (TRA) and comparison of existing trust models with respect to TRA and Function Requirements (FR) of IoT. Furthermore, this survey categorizes and compares existing trust models with respect to their resiliency against TRA, including: attack on node, attack on service, and attack on communication path. Moreover, a conceptual framework has been proposed which shows the minimum requirements to make a successful trust model for smart IoT environment.The paper is available at https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jnca.2019.02.024.

NCSAEL PUBLISHES RESEARCH WORK IN JCR Q1 Journal FGCS, Elsevier (IF 4.639)

NCSAEL has published its first research work entitled “Framework for calculating Return On Security Investment (ROSI) for Security-Oriented organizations” in Future Generations Computer Systems (FGCS), Elsevier (Journal’s Impact Factor: 4.639). We would like to congratulate NCSAEL’s Research Associate, Ms. Tahreem Yaqoob, Director NCSAEL, Dr. Haider Abbas, Dr. Faisal Amjad, Narmeen Shafqat and Azka Arshad on getting the research work published in a JCR - Q1 Journal. The researchers have proposed a comprehensive framework to measure ROSI effectively by overcoming gaps in the traditional approaches. The framework has also been validated with the help of Common Vulnerability Security System (CVSS) attack dataset. By adopting this framework and investing in Cyber security in general, organizations can make effective use of their resources, time and money. Available online : https://doi.org/10.1016/j.future.2018.12.033.

NCSAEL PUBLISHES RESEARCH WORK IN World's most prestigeous Journal IEEE Comm S&T (IF 20.23)

National Cyber Security Auditing and Evaluation Lab (NCSAEL), MCS-NUST proudly announces the publication of its research work entitled “Security Vulnerabilities, Attacks, Countermeasures, and Regulations of Networked Medical Devices" authored by NCSAEL’s Research Associate, Ms. Tahreem Yaqoob, Director NCSAEL, Assoc. Prof. Dr. Haider Abbas, and Prof Dr. Mohammed Atiquzzaman in World's most prestigious Computer Science Journal, “IEEE Communications Surveys and Tutorials” (IEEE COM S&T, Impact Factor: 20.23). This research presents a comprehensive analysis of security vulnerabilities present in more than a hundred medical devices by studying security tests and demonstrating cyber-attacks. The research work also identifies gaps present in implemented technical controls and medical regulations managing these devices. The paper is available online at https://doi.org/10.1109/COMST.2019.2914094.
