Our Mission

Higher Education Comission (HEC) and Planning Commission of Pakistan's sponsored, National Cyber Security Auditing and Evaluation Lab (NCSAEL–NUST) is an internationally recognized Cyber-Security Research Lab established at Military College of Signals, NUST. NCSAEL is Pakistan’s first source of expertise in the field of Information Security, typically focused on:

  • Security Assessment and Auditing of foreign and local IT security products/ services.
  • Indigenous Development of specialized tools/ software for carrying out security screening and malware analysis.
  • Capacity Building for Common Criteria Testing Lab (CCTL) through Assessment of security targets.

NCSAEL Services

NCSAEL encompass a holistic approach right from the Design and Development to Security Assessment and Auditing of local and foreign IT Products/Sofwares

Vulnerability
Assessments and Auditing

Determines your vulnerable IT assets as well as remediation solutions for future attack prevention

Indigenous
Development of Auditing tools

Specializes in design and development of secure tools/ softwares for carrying out security evaluation of Networks, Websites, IT systems, etc

Detection
of Zero Day Malwares

NCSAEL is focused on proactive detection of APTs, anomalies, malicious behaviors etc., thereby preventing the risk of ever-escalating cyber-attacks

Penetration Testing

Our team of highly skilled pentesters can perform penetration testing of your organization's network, websites and other critical services

Training and Workshops

Conduct Information Security trainings and workshops in the domain of Ethical Hacking, Penetration Testing, Security Auditing etc

Internship Programs

NCSAEL’s Internship programs help students gain first-hand knowledge regarding Information Security and use this knowledge for the benefit of Pakistan

READINESS AND PREPAREDNESS FOR CCTL

We help you prepare for the international Common Criteria Product Evaluation to move up the information security maturity curve easily
