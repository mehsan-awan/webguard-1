OIC - CERT (Cyber Emergency Response Team) Cyber Drill


OIC - CERT (Cyber Emergency Response Team) Cyber Drill was held on 17 September, 2019 in Islamabad under the arrangements of Pakistan Information Security Association (PISA). Total 16 x OIC countries participated in Cyber Drill and the event was hosted by State of Oman. From Pakistan, different teams participated. Total of 6 x Injects/Tasks were given to the participating countries. NCSAEL Pakistan responded to all given 6 x Injects. By the grace of Allah Almighty, at the end of the event, Pakistan declared first among 16 x countries and team NCSAEL stood first among all Pakistani participants. Event was covered by national as well as international media.
