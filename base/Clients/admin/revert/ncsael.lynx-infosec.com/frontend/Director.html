I am wearing many hats and currently hold the positions of Head of Information Security Department at the Military College of Signals(MCS) at NUST, Founder and Director for National Cyber Security Auditing and Evaluation Lab, Founder and President of Lynx Information Security (Pvt. Ltd), Regional Editor for Neural Computing and Applications and Full time Associate Editor for several International Journals published by IEEE, Springer and Elsevier . I am also serving as Adjunct Faculty/Doctoral Studies Advisor for some universities in EU, USA and China. I have received professional trainings and certifications from Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT), United States, Stockholm University, Sweden, Stockholm School of Entrepreneurship, Sweden, IBM , USA and Certified Ethical Hacker from EC-Council. I completed my MS in Engineering and Management of Information Systems (2006) and PhD in Information Security (2010) from KTH - Royal Institute of Technology, Stockholm Sweden.
